NYC Housing Maintenance data shows the Bronx has the most complaints

By Evelin Fajardo-Alvarez

December 15, 2023

The Bronx exhibits the most housing maintenance code complaints within the last year according to HPD data. In 2023 from January to November the NYC Housing Preservation and Development department reported 133,209 complaints in the Bronx alone. Coming in second is the borough of Brooklyn at 111,639 complaints this year. Complaints can vary from lack of heat or hot water, signs of lead paint, rodents or pests and many other housing code infractions.
